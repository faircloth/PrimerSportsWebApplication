Football has diversity issues - at all levels

There will be just 12 African-American college football coaches next year, down from 17 in 2011. Because the NCAA does not govern university hiring practices, it is powerless to implement a rule to address the decline. An advocacy group is asking schools to voluntarily commit to interviewing at least one minority candidate for coaching vacancies.
